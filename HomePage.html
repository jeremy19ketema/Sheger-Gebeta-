Featured Review

Sosha

Sosha is a 24/7 health-focused restaurant in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, known for fresh, nutritious meals made with local ingredients.
It offers a variety of healthy dishes like salads, wraps, and juices, promoting a clean, flavorful lifestyle.
Sosha is more than food; it’s a way of life centered on nutrition and care
